This is the easiest part of travel and cheap to park at. Go on line pick your days and times, add in a coupon and if a member you can use points. Points add up to free days, sign up its worth it. You have the option to park inside or outside. Once you park the shuttle bus picks you up at your car and the driver loads your baggage. Then he delivers you to the front door at the airport. Bring some cash to tip the driver. When you return call them on number provided and they show up in fifteen minutes and back to your car. Simple, easy and affordable.
