You know you're coming to this station when the track starts to widen and you enter a covered area with many other trains and platforms. You can tell this station is the center (and the end) for many suburban trains because it is much bigger and exciting. This station is particularly more exciting than other train stations because it has several shops and places to eat. There are also big, readable signs telling you what track the your train is on. On the tracks, there are signs that give a list of all the stops that the train goes to so you can be extra sure you're on the right train.
