Prepare to be whisked away to a time of opulence and splendor.  This long standing institution knows how to treat it's guests.  This is not your average hotel.  From the lux decor from an era gone by, to the afternoon high tea, this is the real deal.  Rooms are basic but comfortable and clean.  The bartenders are institutions in and of themselves and you must stop for a drink.   Within walking distance of the famed Michigan Avenue, and everything else can be easily traveled to with the help of the amazing concierge personal and the bellhops.
